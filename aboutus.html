MAA story

Antonella, Majda and Apolline

MAA is the story of 3 young ladies who met all across the world back in 2017. Bearly 18 those 3 fierce young women met in the United States while they were doing their first internship. From those two exciting summer months came up an incredible friendship based on a common passion; fashion. Dreamer and ambitious those dynamic students decided they wanted to create something together, a special project that will link them forever. Aware of the different societal issues of their world they decided to create a small brand at their image to contribute at their own scale to the improvement of their society. A brand which will be trendy, which will enable women to feel powerful and embrace their best self and which will respect both the environment and the people. Four years, a few up and downs, a lot of sweat and ambition later MAA finally came to life. This brand is the combination of 3 unique personalities and a shared passion… The perfect recipe that we have been sharing with you for months.
